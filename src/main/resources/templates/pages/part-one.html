Version of Windows

there are different versions of Windows that people might have, so it is important to note that I will be using Windows 11. Although There shouldn't be any differences with my approach to emptying your recycle bin.

The first step

There are two direct ways to get to your recycle bin and the first one is to just double-click the recycle bin icon on your desktop screen. The second way to get to your recycle bin is to press your Windows key on your keyboard, or click the Windows logo on your screen, and type recycle bin and when it shows up you click it or hit enter.
